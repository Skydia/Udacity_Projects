Ford GoBike User Behavior¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I would like to see the behavior of customers based on user type, station location, distance, duration, and months.

Dataset Overview¶

This dataset consisted of 519700 rows and 13 columns. Data types are mixed including object, integer, float, and datetime. There are 272 Stations with 3673 bikes. On the 'user_type' column, there are two unique values which are 'Customer' and 'Subscriber'. 8.91% rows were removed due to outliers and unwanted conditional variable.

Distribution of Rental Duration in 2017¶

Duration in this dataset is from 0 to 80k+ seconds. The interquartile range is between 382 and 938. X-Axis scale is changed into logarithmic scale, therefore it's seen as normal distribution.

Duration and Distance by User Type¶

Customer type tends to rent longer than Subscriber. This is shown by all kind of plots including its average. Customer type also tends to have higher distance as well based Box Plot and Bar Chart. While from the Violin Plot, it tells us that the distribution is similar between two user types, except that Subscriber Type have a larger range.
The duration_sec distribution of Customer type seems to be more distributed too, compared to Subscriber type that have a lower median.

Distance by Month for Each User Type¶

This tells us differently compared to the previous plot. It tends to get higher every month based on the above plot. This plot shows that people doesn't ride far in the summer season.
In the Autumn season, people would likely to ride further compared to in the summer season. But this doesn't prove on the duration though. In average, people tends to ride in about 15 minutes no matter in which months.
